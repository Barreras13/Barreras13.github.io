My Grandfather

Here's a time line of my Grandpa's life:

  • 1940 - Born somewhere in Abra
  • 1954 - Left home with his uncle to work somewhere in Benguet as a construction worker
  • 1976 - Became a taxi driver at Baguio City
  • 1980 - Went to Mountain Province to find some work
  • 1983 - He met my grandma at Bauko, Mountain Province
  • 1984 - Forced himself to be religious to get the love of grandma
  • 1985 - They had a child which was my mother
  • 1986 - Became a gardener
  • 1999 - Went back at being a taxi driver in Baguio City
  • 2010 - Got sick with lung cancer
  • 2015 - dies at the age of 75.
